About us


Hi! I’m Jeroen, a Dutchie who’s currently living in Australia and a big fan of Nepal! In 2016 I went on a 2 year world trip and visited 16 countries. Initially I wasn’t planning to go to Nepal but I had to escape the craziness of India and took the bus to Nepal. This was the best decision of my trip. Nepal feels (especially compared to India) like heaven. I stayed 3 months in Nepal and sadly had to leave because my visa ran out. Nepal turned out to be my favorite country in the world. The beautiful nature, mountains and the extremely friendly people makes it for me the best place to go backpacking.

During my trip I got a lot of questions from people about Nepal. That’s what made me decide to make a website about it. 90% of the content is based on my own experiences and the rest I got from other backpackers. Hopefully it will help you with your travels. Feel free to contact me if you’ve any suggestions/questions. You can reach me on [email]. Happy travels!
